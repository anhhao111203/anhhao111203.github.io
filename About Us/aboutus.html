Who We Are
Anh Hao, the founder, and a few of the colleagues founded CYPCYF in 2022 with the simple conviction that our pets live the longest and healthiest lives when given a consistent, nutritious, and well-balanced diet. Every product we supply in our stores has undergone extensive testing to guarantee that it is both healthy and safe for our pets. Every food we provide has received a 4 or 5 star rating from a reputable rating agency.

So don't be hesitant to purchase our pets from my shop.



What We Believe
All of us at CYPCYF hope you enjoy your shopping and always look forward to your product suggestions and feedback - we're here for you! We know how important your pet is to you and that so many of you believe just as we do.
